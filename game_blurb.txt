Game that blatantly rips off breaking bad. "Candy" tycoon for Roblox.

Main game loop:

    Invest money into equipment and resources to make Candy.
    Do research for better equipment and end products.
    Manage your public image, by either being very notorious or very secretive.
    Manage your relation with the "CEA" - the "Candy Enforcement Administration" and avoid getting cought by them.
    Different characters bring different bonuses:

        The Professor: Master in candy manufacturing.
        The Manager: Master in logistics fulfillment.
        The Lawyer: Master in law and CEA relation.
        The Chemist: Master in research.
        The Entrepenour: Extra opportunities reveal themselves to this character.
